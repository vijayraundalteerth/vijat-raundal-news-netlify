According to Mr. Vijay Raundal, his early career was deeply rooted in structured infrastructure work. Government contracting and PWD projects follow a clear process developers receive a tender, execute the work, and deliver it within defined timelines.

However, moving into real estate development brought a completely different set of responsibilities. Unlike infrastructure projects, real estate requires developers to manage multiple aspects independently, including land acquisition, design planning, financial management, and market demand.

This transition was challenging, but the discipline and execution-focused approach that Raundal developed during his infrastructure career helped him successfully adapt to the complexities of the real estate sector.

How Infrastructure Experience Shaped Teerth Realties’ Vision

Insights shared in Mr. Vijay Raundal news often emphasize how his background in infrastructure played a major role in shaping the early vision of Teerth Realties.

Working on roads and public infrastructure gave Raundal a clear understanding of how cities expand over time. Instead of focusing only on infrastructure execution, he recognized an opportunity to contribute to urban development by creating well-planned spaces for businesses and communities.

During this period, Pune was rapidly transforming from an industrial center into a growing IT and education hub. This shift created increasing demand for modern commercial buildings and organized residential projects, encouraging the formation and growth of Teerth Realties.

The Evolution of Pune’s Real Estate Market

Over the last two decades, Pune has experienced significant changes in its real estate landscape. Earlier, the city was largely known for its manufacturing and industrial base. Today, it has emerged as one of India’s leading technology and education hubs.

As highlighted in recent Mr. Vijay Raundal news, the property market has also evolved along with this economic transformation. Buyers and tenants today are more informed and analytical when making property decisions.

Factors such as construction quality, regulatory compliance, infrastructure connectivity, and long-term investment value have become key priorities for both homebuyers and commercial tenants.

Despite these changes, some fundamentals remain constant—Pune’s strong talent pool, favorable climate, and steady economic growth continue to support the city’s long-term real estate potential.

Why Grade-A Commercial Developments Are Gaining Importance

One of the defining aspects of Teerth Realties’ development strategy has been its focus on Grade-A commercial projects.

In many Mr. Vijay Raundal news discussions, he explains that the goal is not just to build structures but to create long-lasting assets that meet modern corporate requirements. Grade-A commercial developments provide advanced infrastructure, efficient design, improved safety systems, and a better working environment.

Such developments also offer stability and reliable returns for investors, which is why they have become increasingly attractive in Pune’s evolving commercial real estate market.

Changing Expectations of Office Buyers and Corporate Tenants

The expectations of office buyers and tenants have also changed significantly in recent years.

Earlier, companies primarily focused on location and office space size. Today, businesses expect much more from commercial developments. Modern offices must offer strong connectivity, energy-efficient buildings, adequate parking, safety systems, and amenities that enhance employee productivity.

According to Mr. Vijay Raundal, office spaces are no longer just workplaces—they are part of the overall employee experience and corporate identity.

Location and Connectivity Remain the Most Critical Factors

Among all real estate factors, location continues to be the most critical element in determining the success of a commercial project.

Even the best-designed buildings can struggle if they are located in poorly connected areas. Access to highways, metro routes, residential neighborhoods, and daily conveniences significantly impacts the value and usability of commercial developments.

For this reason, location planning remains a top priority in the development strategy followed by Teerth Realties.

Managing Real Estate Cycles and Market Slowdowns

Real estate is naturally cyclical, with periods of rapid growth followed by slower market phases. Managing financial risk during these cycles requires careful planning.

Insights highlighted in Mr. Vijay Raundal news indicate that long-term developers focus on financial discipline, phased project execution, and controlled borrowing to navigate market fluctuations.

Maintaining consistent project execution even during slower periods helps build credibility and long-term trust among customers and investors.

Sustainability Becoming Central to Modern Development

Sustainability has become an essential part of modern real estate planning. Developers today are increasingly focusing on energy-efficient design, natural ventilation, water management systems, and environmentally responsible construction practices.

Raundal believes that sustainability not only supports environmental goals but also reduces long-term operational costs for businesses and tenants.

As cities continue to grow, sustainable development will play a major role in shaping the future of commercial real estate.
